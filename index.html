Questions

FAQ – read this before you book a call.

Do I need sales experience? + −

No. We care far more about your work ethic, coachability, and attitude. We’ll train you on our systems and scripts if you bring effort and honesty.

How is pay structured? + −

Compensation is primarily commission-based and may include performance incentives depending on the campaign. There are no guaranteed earnings – your results depend on your execution, consistency, and performance.

Is door-to-door actually safe and legit? + −

Door-to-door is hard work, but when run correctly it’s a legitimate, regulated sales channel. We train on professionalism, ethics, and how to operate respectfully in every neighborhood.

What does a typical day look like? + −

Training and meetings earlier in the day, then several focused hours on doors in the afternoon and evening. It’s intense, but structured so you’re not just wandering around hoping.

Can I do this part-time? + −

Some campaigns have flexibility, but this is not a casual side hustle. The people who win here treat it like a real commitment. We’ll talk through your schedule on the call.

What’s the next step if I’m interested? + −

Simple: book a call using the link on this page. We’ll talk through your goals, answer questions, and decide together if The Underground is the right move for you.
